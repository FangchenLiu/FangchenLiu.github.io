Bio

I am now a senior student in Peking University, majoring in Computer Science. I joined Institute of Digital Media in 2016, and have been working with Professor Yizhou Wang. Piror to that, I was a research intern at SenseTime Group Limited, working on face recognition with Dr. Yi Sun and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. I did my junior summer research in University of California, Berkeley, with Trevor Darrell. We worked on large scale driving dataset and 3D reconstruction. I am currently a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia, Visual Computing Group working with Bo Xin, mainly on reinforcement learning and video game playing.

My research mainly focuses on machine learning and computer vision. I'm interested in scene understanding topics, such as object detection and instance segmentation, which are classic problems but still need well-designed models for further development. Besides, I'm willing to explore some intersections between vision and reinforcement learning, which can also be combined with robotics, such as path planning and decision making.
